History

Durham House of Pets was founded in 1998, by Kevin Calley, when he recognized a demand for pet products and services in the local area that had yet to be satisfied. In the early stages of the business Kevin was the only full time employee of the business and primarily was just a grooming service in which you would drop your pet off in the morning, and pick it up at the end of the day after he or she had been pampered. Over the past 15 years DHP has grown immensely. Kevin now has 15 full time employees and has struck deals with some of the biggest brand names in the pet industry to be a retailer of their goods. Today DHP is a one-stop shop when it comes to caring for your pets with everything from food to the latest and greatest toys right in stock.

Why Use Us

Our loyal customer base we have built over the years we credit to the DHP experience and atmosphere. We take pride in connecting with our client's and their furry best friends on a personal level. Not only do we remember our client’s names and many parts of their personal lives but also their pets. We know the pets favorite treats, what he or she lives for toys while they are waiting to be catered to and more. Kevin realized many years ago the importance gaining the trust of your client as they in return are trusting you with their best friend.
